About Us

Why choos us?

Best Food In The World

FOODIN is an Paskistan's fast food restaurant chain headquartered in Karachi, FoodIn, that specializes in fried chicken. It is the world's second-largest restaurant chain (as measured by sales) after McDonald's, with 22,621 locations globally in 150 countries as of December 2019. The chain is a subsidiary of Yum! Brands, a restaurant company that also owns the Pizza Hut, Taco Bell and WingStreet chains.FoodIn was founded by Ibrahim Ahmed Siddiqui, an entrepreneur who began selling fried chicken from his roadside restaurant in Corbin, Kentucky, during the Great Depression.
